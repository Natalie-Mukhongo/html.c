RECIPE FOR PILAU

Ingredients

1KG Rice

1/2 kg beef

Vegetable oil

Salt

4 onions

4 tomatoes

Garlic

Ginger

Tomato paste

Pilau Masala

Procedure

Chop the beef into small beef cubes. Boil the beef under medium heat for 30 minutes. Once boiled, set aside for later.

Chop the onions into small circular shapes.

Peel the garlic cloves and grind the cloves to make a paste.

Peel the ginger and grind into a paste.

Chop the tomatoes into circular shapes.

Take half a cup of vegetable oil and add it to a cooking pot. Heat the oil under medium heat.

Add the onions and saute till golden brown.

Add the ginger and garlic paste. Saute for five minutes.

Add the chopped tomatoes and saute into a fine paste.

Add 2 tablespoons of salt and 3 tablespoons of Pilau masala. Mix the spices well.

Add 1 can of tomato paste and mix.

Add the boiled beef and mix well with the spices. Allow it to cook for about 15 minutes.

To the beef add three glasses of rice and mix till the rice achieves the brown colour.

To the rice, add 6 cups of hot water and mix.

Cover the meal and let it cook under low heat.

Check the meal at 15 minutes intervals, mixing evrytime to avoid the rice from sticking at the bottom of the cooking pot.

Once there is no more water, the Pilau is ready.

Serve with guacamole.
